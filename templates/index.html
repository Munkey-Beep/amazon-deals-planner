Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes, completely free. No hidden fees, no premium tier, no credit card required.

What CSV files do I need?

Two exports from Seller Central: your Manage Inventory report and your Fee Preview report. Both are standard Amazon exports.

Does it work for any brand?

Yes. The tool auto-detects your product categories and works with any Amazon FBA brand, any marketplace.

How are fees calculated?

Upfront fees are charged once per deal period and deducted from total sales. Variable fees are charged per unit sold. We support all 13 Amazon promotion types.
