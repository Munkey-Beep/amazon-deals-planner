The Problem

Amazon deals are profitable — if you calculate the fees right.

Most sellers run Lightning Deals and Best Deals without knowing their actual margins. The fee structure is complex, and one wrong number wipes out your profit.

😤

Upfront fees eat into revenue

Lightning Deals cost $500+ upfront. That's deducted from your total sales, not per unit. Most sellers get this wrong.

📉

Variable fees add up fast

Every unit sold in a deal has an additional variable fee on top of your normal FBA fees. Easy to overlook, hard to recover.

📅

Peak events need planning

Prime Day, BFCM, and other peak events have different fee structures. Planning last minute is leaving money on the table.

🔢

Spreadsheets take hours

Building your own calculator means hours of manual work every time you have a new SKU lineup or deal cycle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes, completely free. No hidden fees, no premium tier, no credit card required. Just upload your files and download your planner.

What files do I need?

The Deals Recommendation Template (Excel .xlsx) is the main file — Amazon pre-fills it with your recommended deals. Get it from Seller Central → Deals → Manage Deals → Bulk Upload. Optionally add your Fee Preview CSV (Seller Central → Reports → Fulfillment → Fee Preview) for referral + FBA fee accuracy.

What does the tool actually calculate?

For each deal type, it calculates the upfront fee (deducted once from total deal revenue), the per-unit variable fee, your net revenue after fees and COGS, and your projected profit at different sell-through rates.

Does it work outside the US?

Yes. The tool supports US, Canada, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Japan, Australia, Mexico, and other marketplaces.

Is my data secure?

Your CSV data is used only to generate your planner and is not stored or shared. The Excel file is generated instantly and sent directly to your browser.

Why is it slow to load sometimes?

The app runs on free hosting that sleeps when idle. If it's your first visit in a while, it may take ~30 seconds to wake up. Just wait a moment and try again.
